Our Story

Discover the journey of our church from humble beginnings to a thriving community

Founder and Lead
Pastor & his Wife

Founded in 2009, the Assembly of God Church in Ruwanwella has been a beacon of hope and faith in our community for nearly five decades. What began as a small gathering in a local home has grown into a vibrant congregation serving the spiritual needs of our town and surrounding areas.

Our journey has been marked by God's faithfulness through seasons of growth and challenge. We've witnessed countless lives transformed by the power of the Gospel and have had the privilege of serving our community through various outreach programs.

Today, we continue our mission to make disciples of Jesus Christ, equipped to serve God and others with love and compassion. Our doors are open to all who seek spiritual truth, community, and a deeper relationship with God.

We believe in the power of prayer, the authority of Scripture, and the transforming work of the Holy Spirit. Our church is committed to being a place where people can encounter God, grow in faith, and discover their purpose in Christ.
