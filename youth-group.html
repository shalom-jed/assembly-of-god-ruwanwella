Our Ministries

Youth Group

A dynamic community for teens and young adults to encounter Jesus, build real friendships, and discover their purpose in God.

About the Ministry

Our Youth Group is a place where young people can be real, ask questions, and grow in their relationship with God together.

We believe God is raising up a generation of young men and women who will shine for Jesus in their schools, families, and workplaces.

Meeting Details

What We Do

  • Youth worship, prayer, and Bible teaching
  • Small group discussions and mentoring
  • Games, fun activities, and youth outings
  • Youth involvement in Sunday services
  • Community service and outreach projects
