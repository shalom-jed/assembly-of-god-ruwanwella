Giving & Generosity

Support the Ministry of Assembly of God Church, Ruwanwella

Your faithful giving helps us preach the Gospel, care for families, support our members, and build God’s house in Ruwanwella and beyond.

Where Your Gift Goes

Please choose a fund and use the fund name as your payment reference when you give via bank transfer or deposit.

Tithes & Offerings

Supports the day-to-day ministry of the church: worship services, discipleship, outreach, and pastoral care.

  • Helps cover church utilities and ministry expenses
  • Enables ongoing outreach and evangelism

Building Fund

Invest in the current church building and future developments in Ruwanwella.

  • Supports construction, maintenance, and improvements
  • Helps create a welcoming place of worship

Members’ Welfare Fund

Assists church members and families in times of need, crisis, or emergency.

  • Supports urgent needs
  • Administered with pastoral oversight and accountability

Payment Reference

When you make a transfer, please add a note or reference so we know which fund you are giving to:

  • Tithes – “Tithes”
  • Offering – “Offering”
  • Building Fund – “Building Fund”
  • Welfare – “Welfare”

If you are giving to more than one fund in a single transfer, you can write a short description (e.g. “Tithes + Building”) and also inform the pastor or treasurer.
