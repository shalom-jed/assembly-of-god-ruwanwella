Children's Ministry

Helping children know, love, and follow Jesus in a safe, joyful, and age-appropriate environment.

About the Ministry

Our Children's Ministry provides a fun and welcoming place where kids can learn about God, experience His love, and grow in faith.

We believe children are a precious gift from the Lord and a vital part of the church today, not just the church of tomorrow.
