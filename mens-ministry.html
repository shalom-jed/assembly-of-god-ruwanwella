Our Ministries

Men's Ministry

Building godly men who lead their families, serve the church, and impact the community through the power of Jesus Christ.

About the Ministry

Our Men's Ministry focuses on spiritual growth, accountability, and practical service. Men gather to study the Word, pray together, and encourage one another.

We believe strong men of God are vital for strong families, a strong church, and a strong community.

Meeting Details

  • When: Every 2nd Saturday at 8:00 AM
  • Where: AOG Church, Ruwanwella Estate
  • Leader: Brother Nimal Perera

What We Do

  • Monthly breakfast and fellowship
  • Bible study and prayer
  • Visitation and practical help for families in need
  • Support for church events and outreach
